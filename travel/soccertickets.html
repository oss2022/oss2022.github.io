Soccer (including tickets to matches)

    Madrid is world-known for two of its professional football teams, Real Madrid (14 times UEFA Champions Leage winner) and Atlético de Madrid.

  • Football fans won't want to miss a visit to the magnificent Santiago Bernabéu, home to the legendary Real Madrid football team. If so, you should do the Bernabéu Tour which takes around 60 minutes and costs from 12 to 15 €.
  • Apart from visiting the Santiago Bernabéu Stadium, attendees can also visit the new Wanda Metropolitano Stadium and see its facilities, inaugurated in September 2017. The Wanda Metropolitano hosted the final game of the UEFA European Champions League in May 2019.

    Getting tickets to watch Real Madrid matches is not easy (and certainly expensive). If you want to attend one match of the Spanish LaLiga at the Bernabéu, you should buy tickets well in advance, as tickets get sold-out fast. However, the definite date and time of the match is finally set just three weeks in advance, and could be on Saturday or on Sunday.

    Another option is to try to get tickets using the secondary marketplace onlineticketexpress.com or the ticket resale site Viagogo (see Wikipedia page on Viagogo).
