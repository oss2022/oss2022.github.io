Lunches

All days between 12:30 and 14:00

    Spain is known for its good taste and variety of food. OSS 2022 could not be different and we have organized some special arrangements so that participants enjoy not only the program, but good food in a nice surrounding and (hopefully) good weather.

    Lunch will be served in the Las Bóvedas de Cibeles Restaurant, crossing Alcalá street. Instead of a standing cocktail, we have chosen a typical Spanish restaurant with a special taste, where the menú del día can be appreciated, which includes two courses (with three choices each, one vegetarian/vegan) and dessert. Conference participants may accompany their meal with local wine and beer, if desired (and at no cost).
